Warriors are awesome! They're like strong humans with massive swords, who protect you, but they're also buff af and you want just get embraced by those big bulky arms, and now I'm dreaming, thank you very much!
Ninjas are the sneaky bastards, you either go double-sword, swish swish monsters, or you sneak behind them and go swish swish with a bow and some arrows! And how can't you not like that?!
Suras? They're the scary dudes who you don't mess around with because they show you their scary monster arm and you're going to pass out, because that's how much they scared the pants off of me when I was a kid, like look at them! And I dare you not to be scared!
Shamans are my dudes, always played them, always will. Girls are slim, guys are buff, but even the buffest shaman won't be scared about his masculinity when he parades around with the awesome fan and/or bell, like they have extinguished the idea of toxic masculinity from my adolescence.
See this dude? He's a dude, but he's a wolf, I don't know much about his kind, but man, do I have to? He's a bloody werewolf of the Metin world? He must be tough, rough and all the big bad qualities you need in a guy to fight monsters with. I like him so much, I'm gonna get the game again, nostalgia style, and choose him as my dude. I'm going to name him Scruffles.
